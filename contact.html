Common Questions
How do I apply for a loan?
Click "Apply for Loan" in the nav, fill the 4-step form with your NID, purpose, and references. The committee reviews within 14 days.
Is the loan really 0% interest?
Yes — absolutely zero interest. You repay only the principal amount. This is the core of Qard al-Hasan in Islam.
How is my donation used?
90% goes directly to the loan pool, 5% covers operations, and 5% is kept as an emergency reserve. All tracked publicly.
Can I get a tax rebate certificate?
Yes. After donating, visit the Donation page and use the "Get Certificate" button to download your tax rebate certificate for any fiscal year.
