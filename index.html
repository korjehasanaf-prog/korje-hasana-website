How it works

Simple, transparent, dignified

Four steps — from your first application to receiving what you need, with respect and care at every stage.

1
Apply online or in person
Fill out the application form on this website or WhatsApp us. Upload your NID card — fields fill automatically.
2
Verification visit
Our team visits your home or workplace to verify the need and meet your references.
3
Committee approval
The loan committee reviews and approves within 7–14 days. You receive an SMS notification.
4
Goods disbursed directly
We purchase the goods or pay the bills on your behalf. You repay only the principal — zero interest, forever.
